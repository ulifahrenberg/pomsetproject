What

The (i)Po(m)set Project is a research project at the crossroads of concurrency theory, algebra, and geometry. It aims to understand the basics of concurrency theory and develop its foundations.

When

The (i)Po(m)set Project started sometime in 2018, but its name and this web site exist only since 21 May 2021.

How

Members and possibly associates meet every Friday afternoon on zoom to discuss research and papers and otherwise banter over big and small things.
