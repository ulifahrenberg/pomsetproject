What

The (i)Po(m)set Project is a research project at the crossroads of concurrency theory, automata theory, algebra, and geometry. It aims to understand the basics of concurrency theory and develop its foundations.

Meetings

The Paris Automata and Concurrency Theory Seminar takes place for an afternoon every six-eight weeks, moving between LIX (École Polytechnique), LRE (EPITA Paris), and IRIF (Université Paris Cité).

The workshops on Pomsets and Related Structures (RaPS) take place irregularly:

  1. Rennes, April 2024
  2. Rzeszów, June 2025
  3. Saclay, September 2025

The Pomset Project Working Group meets twice a month in the Paris region:

  • every first Tuesday at IRIF
  • every third Thursday at LMF

The (i)Po(m)set Project Online Seminar used to take place about once a month, but has now been discontinued.
